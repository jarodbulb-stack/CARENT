## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Business Data**: Properties, tenants, payments, credits, expenses, employee information, water customers, and other business records you enter into the app.
- **Account Information**: If you choose to sign in, we collect your Google account email address for authentication purposes.

### Information Automatically Collected
- **Device Information**: Basic device type for app optimization (stored locally only).
- **Usage Data**: App feature usage patterns (stored locally only).

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:
- Provide the app's core functionality
- Sync your data across devices (if cloud sync is enabled)
- Backup and restore your data
- Improve app performance

---

## 4. Data Storage

### Local Storage (Default)
- All data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB
- Data remains on your device even without internet
- You maintain full control of your data
- You can export/backup your data at any time

### Cloud Storage (Optional - Requires Sign In)
- If you sign in with Google, data syncs to Firebase Cloud Firestore
- Data is stored in Google's secure data centers (Asia-Southeast region)
- Your data is associated with your unique user ID
- Only you can access your data (requires authentication)
